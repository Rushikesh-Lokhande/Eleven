About Eleven Robotics

We build the compute layer that lets robotics teams focus on autonomy, not board spins.

Engineering-First, Robotics-Focused

Eleven Robotics was founded by robotics and embedded engineers who spent years shipping autonomous systems in factories, warehouses, and outdoor environments. After redesigning carrier boards and compute modules again and again, we decided to build the System-on-Modules we always wished we had.

Our SoMs are designed to survive real deployments: temperature swings, vibration, dusty enclosures, and power-constrained mobile platforms. We obsess over thermal design, EMI performance, and long-term availability so you can ship robots with confidence.

What We Focus On

  • Robotics compute modules with long lifecycles
  • Reference carrier designs and integration support
  • Software images tuned for robotics and AI
  • Manufacturing support for fleets and OEMs

Who We Serve

  • Autonomous mobile robot manufacturers
  • Industrial automation and OEMs
  • Research labs and robotics startups
  • Embedded AI and vision system integrators
